Dr. APJ Abdul Kalam

Dr. APJ Abdul Kalam, popularly known as the 'Missile Man of India,' was born on 15th October 1931 in Rameswaram, Tamil Nadu. He served as the 11th President of India from 2002 to 2007. A renowned aerospace scientist, he played a pivotal role in India's missile development and nuclear capabilities.
